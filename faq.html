General FAQs about flying

Why is flying so bad?

But the aviation industry only makes 3% of global emissions so it can’t be that bad, can it?

While making up only a small portion of global emissions, aviation is the sector that is set to expand the fastest, with predictions of it reaching 10% by 2030. The transport sector as a whole is only responsible for 15% of global emissions, so that’s quite a large chunk!

Specific FAQs about the campaign

Why 100,000?

Why only for a year?

Asking people to stop flying forever is unlikely to appeal. But an air-free year can be a good way to break an established habit and enable people to start to see other alternatives. Having a time limit focuses the pledge as well as makes it an achievable aim. Commitment to something in the short term increases the chances of longer-term behavioural change.
